Foundation Chair #6

Collection: The Iris Foundation (IRIS-FOUNDATION)
Token ID: 6
Contract: 0x00008c80D4cBD653B1D384566d9b23B37d100000

Chair #6 is one of 15 functionally identical Genesis Foundation Chairs. On-chain, all Chairs share the same rights and obligations — there are no per-token trait differences, names, or privilege tiers.

On-Chain Powers​

PowerDescription
Fee shareEqual portion of ClaimRewards(token) distributions among live Chairs
Threshold bypassSubmit governance proposals without standard voting threshold
Consul vetoParticipate in 50% + 1 collective veto during timelock
Kamikaze vetoBurn this token to instantly veto a proposal in emergencies

Economic Rights​

When traders close profitable positions, 5% of gross profit (foundationFeeBps = 500) is minted to the Foundation contract. Chair #6's holder receives:

amountPerCard = totalRewards / liveCards

If Chair #6 is burned via Kamikaze veto, it is removed from activeCardsRegistry and permanently forfeits its fee stream. The remaining live Chairs absorb the redistributed share.

Kamikaze Game Theory​

Invoking Kamikaze veto on Chair #6 is rational only when the existential threat to the protocol exceeds the net present value of this Chair's perpetual 1/15 fee stream. For recoverable disputes, Consul veto (coordination without burn) is preferred.
